Privacy Policy

At MyWebClass.org, we are dedicated to preserving your privacy. Our Privacy Policy specifies the type of information we collect from you when you visit our website, how we use it, and the precautions we take to safeguard it. Your use of our website indicates your agreement with the terms set out in this Privacy Policy. Users have the right to opt-out of having their personal data collected or used.

Info Collected

We may collect the following information from you when you visit our website:

  • Your personal information, like your email address and name, if you decide to share it with us.
  • Details about how you use the website, such as the pages you visit, the length of time you spend on the site, and the links you click.
  • Information about your device, like your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Info

We use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • Enhancing our website and optimizing the user experience.
  • Analyzing user behavior and measure the effectiveness of our content and marketing efforts.
  • Communicating with users about our products and services, if you have provided us with your contact information into our system.
  • Adhering to legal obligations.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about your use of our website. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit when they do so, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our website. We use the information we get from Google Analytics to improve our website and the user experience. Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit our website, rather than your name or other identifying information. We do not combine the information collected through the use of Google Analytics with personally identifiable information. Google Analytics plants a permanent cookie on your web browser to identify you as a unique user the next time you visit our website. This cookie cannot be used by anyone other than Google.
